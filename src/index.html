How It Works

From sensor to repair order

Four steps from raw data to a fixed road.

01

Collect

IoT sensors, satellite imagery, vehicle telematics, and citizen reports feed continuous data into the platform.

02

Analyse

AI models process the data stream, detect anomalies, classify surface defects, and calculate risk scores per segment.

03

Prioritise

A dynamic priority queue ranks roads by risk, traffic volume, and repair cost — giving engineers an optimal maintenance plan.

04

Dispatch

Work orders auto-generate and route to repair crews. Progress is tracked to closure and fed back into the model.
